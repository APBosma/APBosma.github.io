Officers

Hopper-turing has five officer positions: President, Vice-President, Treasurer, Secretary, Social Chair, and SGA Representative. The Social Chair plans social events for members to participate in like movie and bowling nights. The Secretary helps keep track of background club activities and maintenance. The Treasurer keeps track of Hopper-Turing's finances. Vice-President and President both work together to make sure that all of these things are done and the club works as normal. The SGA Representative attends SGA meetings and makes sure to let SGA know what is happening in our club.
